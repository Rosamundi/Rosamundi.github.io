Welcome to the profile page of the Rosamundi GitHub repository

Rosamundi is Latin for 'Rose of the world' and is symbolic of both my affinity for the rose and my passion for exploring new worlds

Professional skills:

  • I am a student of Romance Languages and Computer Science. My specialty is the Italian language, and I am currently studying and rapidly gaining an understanding of French
  • I also have a more limited knowledge, primarily of grammar, of Old French, Latin, and Arabic
  • I have a basic understanding, through university courses as well as online tutorials, of several programming languages, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python

Supplementary Experiences

My passion for exploring the differences in how different languages function is conducive to an interest in the cultures of those languages. My first experience abroad consisted of three months living and studying in a fairly rural area of Italy, a region called the Marche.

Hobbies

On the side, I enjoy:
  • Knitting
  • Crocheting
  • Writing fantasy stories

I am fond of fantasy worlds similar to that of the Lord of the Rings, with the exception of my fascination with dragons, who are almost always (although not without exception) protagonists. I prefer the idea of a system like that introduced in Christopher Paolini's Eragon: humans and dragons working together for justice.

As an amalgamation of my passion for language and my fantasy stories, I am working on creating a language for the elves in my stories.
